What is Wallet Balance Protection?

Wallet Balance Protection is a feature offered by PaySprint that ensures your transactions go through even when your wallet lacks sufficient funds. Essentially, it provides overdraft protection, allowing you to continue making payments or completing transactions without any disruptions. This service is particularly useful for businesses, helping to prevent interruptions and ensuring smooth operations, even in situations where immediate funds may be unavailable.

Who is Eligible for PaySprint Wallet Balance Protection?

Not everyone qualifies for Wallet Balance Protection right away. Here's what you need to qualify:

  1. Verified Account: Your account must be fully verified.
  2. Active for 6 Months: You must have maintained an active account on the PaySprint platform for at least six months.

Once you meet these criteria, PaySprint will reach out to offer you the option to enable Wallet Balance Protection, ensuring smooth transactions even with low wallet funds.
